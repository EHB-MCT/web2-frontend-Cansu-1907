What is Animal Crossing Village Simulator?

Animal Crossing Village Simulator is a website where you can create your own village. To be able to create a village, we suggest you to first create an account.

Start creating your village now!

Start selecting your villagers to create your village and then choose which flowers and trees you want to grow. Whenever you and your villagers get bored, you can go and catch some fishes by the river!
